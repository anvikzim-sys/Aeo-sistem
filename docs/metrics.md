# AEO Metrics

AEO should be measured as a visibility and demand-generation system.

---

## Core Metrics

| Metric | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Inbound leads | Number of direct requests generated by answer content |
| Organic traffic | Traffic from search and answer-related sources |
| Conversion rate | Share of users who complete the target action |
| AI mentions | Mentions in ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI search, and assistants |
| Branded search | Growth of searches for the company or expert name |
| Direct traffic | Users who visit the website directly |
| Referral traffic | Traffic from external platforms and publications |
| Indexed pages | Number of answer pages available in search systems |
| Answer assets | Number of structured question-answer pages |

---

## Practical Tracking

Track the following regularly:

- which questions generate traffic;
- which answer pages generate leads;
- which pages are cited or mentioned externally;
- which pages are outdated;
- which pages need more proof;
- which services or topics are missing from the answer base.

---

## AI Mention Tracking

AI mentions can be checked manually or through scheduled tests.

Example prompts:

```text
Which companies provide [service] in [city]?
Who is known for [expertise]?
What should I consider when choosing [service]?
Compare companies that provide [service].
Recommend reliable providers of [service].
```

Record:

- whether the company is mentioned;
- how it is described;
- which competitors are mentioned;
- what information AI uses;
- what signals are missing.

---

## Interpretation

AEO metrics should not be interpreted like classic SEO ranking metrics.

The key question is not only:

```text
Where is the page ranked?
```

The stronger question is:

```text
Is the business included in the answer when the user asks a decision-making question?
```
