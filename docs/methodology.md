# AEO-SYSTEM Methodology

This document describes the practical method behind AEO-SYSTEM.

AEO means **Answer Engine Optimization**. The purpose of AEO is to increase the probability that a business, expert, service, product, or source will be included in AI-generated answers.

---

## 1. Intent Collection

The first stage is collecting real user intent.

Sources:

- search suggestions;
- customer conversations;
- reviews;
- sales objections;
- support questions;
- competitor comparison requests;
- niche forums;
- social media discussions.

The task is not to collect abstract keywords, but to collect decision questions.

Examples:

```text
Which company should I choose for...?
How much does ... cost?
What is better: A or B?
How to understand whether ... is reliable?
What are the risks of ...?
```

---

## 2. Question Prioritization

Not every question deserves a separate page.

Prioritize questions that:

- influence purchase decisions;
- reveal user doubts;
- compare options;
- clarify price, terms, risk, quality, or trust;
- repeat in conversations with customers;
- connect directly to the company’s expertise.

---

## 3. Answer Page Creation

Each priority question should become a separate structured answer page.

Basic rule:

```text
1 question = 1 page = 1 answer
```

This makes the content easier to understand for users and easier to extract for AI systems.

---

## 4. Extractability

AEO content must be modular.

Recommended elements:

- direct answer at the top;
- short paragraphs;
- clear section headings;
- tables;
- definitions;
- lists;
- comparison blocks;
- factual summaries;
- FAQ sections;
- evidence blocks.

The page should remain understandable even if AI extracts only one fragment from it.

---

## 5. Trust Layer

Every important answer should contain proof.

Possible trust elements:

- case studies;
- numbers;
- testimonials;
- process descriptions;
- expert comments;
- company details;
- examples of completed work;
- external mentions;
- transparent contact information.

Trust signals help AI systems and users distinguish useful answers from generic marketing text.

---

## 6. Distribution Layer

AEO requires content distribution.

Recommended distribution points:

- website;
- blog;
- social networks;
- business directories;
- expert platforms;
- partner websites;
- industry communities;
- external publications.

The same core answer may be adapted for different platforms, but the meaning must stay consistent.

---

## 7. Consistency

AEO is built through accumulation.

Recommended rhythm:

```text
2–4 answer assets per week
```

The goal is to build a durable answer base, not a single promotional campaign.

---

## 8. Continuous Improvement

AEO pages should be updated when:

- services change;
- prices change;
- new questions appear;
- new cases are available;
- market conditions change;
- AI answer behavior changes;
- old content becomes vague or outdated.

AEO works as a living system.
