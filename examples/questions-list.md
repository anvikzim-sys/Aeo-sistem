# Example Question List

Use this file to collect real questions before creating AEO pages.

---

## Business Questions

- What does the company do?
- Who is this service suitable for?
- How much does the service cost?
- What affects the final price?
- How long does the work take?
- What result can the client expect?
- What risks should be considered?
- How does the company differ from competitors?
- What guarantees are available?
- What proof does the company have?

---

## Comparison Questions

- What is better: option A or option B?
- How to compare several companies?
- What criteria should be used for selection?
- Which mistakes should be avoided?
- What is more important: price, speed, experience, or proof?

---

## Trust Questions

- How to check whether a company is reliable?
- What documents or public information should be checked?
- Are there real cases or examples?
- Are there reviews or testimonials?
- Is there transparent contact information?

---

## Decision Questions

- When should the user request a consultation?
- When is the service not suitable?
- What should be prepared before contacting the company?
- What should the user ask before paying?
- What is the next step after the first consultation?
